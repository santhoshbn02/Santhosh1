Karnataka Top 10 Tourist Places

1. Jog Falls

Jog Falls is the highest waterfall in Karnataka located in Shivamogga district.

2. Mysore Palace

Mysore Palace is one of the most famous historical palaces in India.

3. Hampi

Hampi is a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its ancient temples and ruins.

4. Coorg

Coorg is a beautiful hill station famous for coffee plantations and nature.

5. Chikmagalur

Chikmagalur is known for hills, coffee estates and peaceful environment.

6. Gokarna

Gokarna is famous for its temples and beautiful beaches.

7. Udupi

Udupi is famous for Sri Krishna Temple and delicious Udupi cuisine.

8. Agumbe

Agumbe is known as the Cherrapunji of South India and famous for sunsets.

9. Badami

Badami is famous for rock cut cave temples and historical monuments.

10. Kabini

Kabini is a wildlife destination known for forest safari and animals.
