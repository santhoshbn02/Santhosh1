Jog Falls

Jog Falls is one of the highest waterfalls in India. It is located in the Shivamogga district of Karnataka. The waterfall is formed by the Sharavathi River and has a height of about 253 meters. Jog Falls is a famous tourist destination and attracts thousands of visitors every year.

The best time to visit Jog Falls is during the monsoon season when the water flow is very strong and beautiful. It is surrounded by green forests and hills which make the place very scenic.
